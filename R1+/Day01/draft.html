2qb's 100 Days of Code: Day 01

(Front-end web development)

Who am I?

Hi, I'm 2qb. I'm currently a Computer Science student at VNU-HCM High School for the Gifted.

I am currently taking the 100 Days of Code challenge to improve my coding skills and get ready for my future jobs and volunteer work.

My targets for this challenge

As I take part in this challenge, I am looking forward to these things in each phase:

Phase 1

  • Better understanding of Computer Science
  • Intermediate knowledge of object-oriented C++
  • Intermediate knowledge of front-end web development
  • Intermediate knowledge of Spigot plugin development

Phase 2

  • Good foundation of Computer Science, specifically on data structures and algorithms
  • Intermediate knowledge of full-stack web development
  • Mastery in Spigot plugin development

Why "phases"?

As a high school student, I have to prepare for exams and other stuff, and the main reason this challenge had to be split into 2 phases is because I have a huge chunk of final exams in the middle.

My plans after this challenge

Surprisingly enough, this is just Round 1 of possibly many, many rounds of the challenge. Here are my priorities in Round 2:

  1. Focus on C# (as that's what we're studying at school next year)
  2. Continuation on web development (as part of my school's "Entropy Challenge" game show)
  3. Start working on more projects (both open-source ones and personal ones)
